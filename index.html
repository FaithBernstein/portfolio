I'm a video producer for ABC News, chasing stories that inform and inspire our audience of over 20 million. With 11 years of experience and over a billion video views, I have a knack for finding the human side of any story and drawing people in.

I specialize in producing brand-defining features, ideating and launching fresh video series, and getting in the zone in Adobe Premiere to deliver breaking news edits and quick turnarounds — and with a bit more time on projects, crafting emotional narratives and making people feel at ease during on-camera interviews.

Outside of work, you'll find me behind the lens — hiking up mountains, swimming in alpine lakes, looking for rocks in rivers, biking around Seattle, or slowing down with pottery, golden-hour photography, or a trip to the farmers' market.
